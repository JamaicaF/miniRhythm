miniRhythm is an audio step sequencer built with Tone.js, Audacity, and HTML5 canvas.


Make your own electro beats in the browser!


- To get started, click any gray matrix pad.


- Use the 'clear' button to begin a new sequence.


- Click 'demo' to activate old school Detroit techno :)
